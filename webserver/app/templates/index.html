COVID restrictions/Pass Information:

🦠 During your booking, please indicate if you would like to participate in ruedas (rotating partners). If not, you are welcome to stick to one partner for the whole duration of the class.

🦠 ALL rueda participants will need to email a valid NHS COVID Pass to ad971 @ cam.ac.uk , ahead of the class and no later than 8pm on Tuesday. You can get your NHS COVID pass by i) being fully vaccinated in the UK, ii) providing NHS with a negative PCR or rapid lateral flow test within the past 48 hours or iii) providing NHS with a positive PCR test within the past 6 months. Vaccination records from abroad also accepted. Info on how to download your COVID Pass as a PDF here: COVID Pass

🦠 We STRONGLY SUGGEST that, even if fully vaccinated, you take a PCR or rapid lateral flow test 48 hours before the class, to minimize the chances of transmission and help protect our dancing community. Check if you are eligible for the University Asymptomatic COVID-19 Screening Programme here: Programme
FREE rapid lateral flow tests can be ordered here: Lateral Flow Tests

🦠 Please, only join the class if you are not experiencing any COVID’19-related symptoms.
